Things to be aware of upon arrival to the interdimensional portal



One must be mentally and physically prepared when entering the Primrose Hills realm.
What is seen cannot be unseen.

This is a safe place.
No matter where you are on your journey,
feel free to rest for a bit here.
Everyone is welcome to stay as long as they want.

But, stranger, I must warn you,
for the grasp of eternity is not far away.

In our sacred tradition, one must not fear eternity,
for it is the vector through which everything becomes one again.
Rather, embrace it as it comes forward,
and as time works along with us.

Ah! come in already and you will see.

Better to understand,
than to be understood!
